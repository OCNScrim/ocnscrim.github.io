Contributing

Contributing to our plugins is easy, just send us a pull request, and if your code is of acceptable quality, we'll accept it! Make sure you have read the contributing guide before making your pull releases as that makes our job much easier. You can also become an official member of the team by tweeting @EricZeiberg or send a skype message to any one of us. (eric.zeiberg or ShinyDialga45).
